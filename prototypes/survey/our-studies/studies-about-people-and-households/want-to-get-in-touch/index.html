Want to get in touch?

Contact details you need to speak to us about our people, family and household studies.

Getting in touch about our studies

Please contact our freephone enquiry line on +44 (0)800 298 5313 speak to an advisor for help with:

  • making an appointment for the interviewer to call at a time convenient to you
  • getting a message to an interviewer who has already contacted you
  • any other queries you have about our studies or taking part NGT users should dial 18110 before this number when calling.

Telephone opening times are:

  • Monday to Thursday – 9am to 9pm
  • Friday – 9am to 8pm
  • Saturday – 9am to 1pm

Getting in touch about the National Survey for Wales

If you are taking part in the National Survey for Wales, and would like to be interviewed in Welsh, please contact freephone number +44 (0)800 496 to arrange this in advance.

If you have any other queries about the National Survey for Wales, please call freephone +44 (0)800 298 5313.

Telephone opening times are:

  • Monday to Thursday – 9am to 9pm
  • Friday – 9am to 8pm
  • Saturday – 9am to 1pm

Getting in touch about the Crime Survey for England and Wales

For the Crime Survey for England and Wales, please ring the Crime Survey Information Line at Kantar Public on +44 (0)800 051 0882.

Telephone opening times are:

  • Monday to Friday – 9am to 9pm
  • Saturday – 9am to 12pm

Share your suggestions with us

We’d appreciate your suggestions on how we can improve any of our studies. It would also be great to hear from you if you’re happy with the service you’ve received. You can email us at surveyfeedback@ons.gov.uk.
